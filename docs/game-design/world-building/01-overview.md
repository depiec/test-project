# World Building Overview

## Setting the Scene

The game takes place in **Aethelgard**, a dark fantasy world inspired by the Netherworld from the "Overlord" anime. The player awakens as a powerful entity, resurrected in a new world where the laws of magic and reality are different from their previous existence.

## Player Character

The player is **The Overlord** - an ancient and immensely powerful entity with abilities beyond normal mortal understanding. Resurrected in the Great Tomb of Nazarick, the player possesses:

- **Divine Strength**: Strength that can crush stone and metal
- **Necromantic Mastery**: The ability to raise and command undead armies
- **Dark Magic**: Access to powerful forbidden magics
- **Charismatic Influence**: The ability to inspire fear and loyalty in others

## Key Races

### Undead Races
- **Skeleton Warriors**: Basic infantry with enhanced durability
- **Zombies**: Swarm units that overwhelm enemies through numbers
- **Wights**: Powerful undead commanders with enhanced abilities
- **Wraiths**: Invisible assassins and scouts
- **Liches**: Magical masters with devastating spellcasting

### Demi-Human Races
- **Dark Elves**: Magically inclined warriors and spellcasters
- **Orcs**: Brutal but disciplined soldiers
- **Goblins**: Small but numerous infiltrators and technicians
- **Trolls**: Massive, regenerating warriors

### Original Races
- **Celestials**: Ancient beings of light and purity
- **Infernals**: Beings of fire and chaos
- **Shadow Kin**: Masters of stealth and darkness

## Factions

### The Overlord's Forces
- **The Great Tomb of Nazarick**: The player's base and stronghold
- **Nazarick Guard**: Elite undead defenders
- **Undead Legion**: The player's expanding army
- **Allied Races**: Demi-humans and other creatures who swear allegiance

### Enemy Factions
- **The Holy Alliance**: Human kingdoms united against the undead
- **The Dark Council**: Rival necromancers seeking power
- **Corrupted Entities**: Monsters twisted by dark magic

## Magic System

The world of Aethelgard operates on a **Tiered Magic System**:

### Tier 1 - Basic Magic
- Elemental manipulation (fire, ice, lightning)
- Simple healing and restoration
- Minor illusions

### Tier 2 - Advanced Magic
- Combat enchantments
- Enhanced healing
- Moderate illusions

### Tier 3 - Divine Magic
- Necromancy and death manipulation
- Powerful enchantments
- Limited resurrection

### Tier 4 - Forbidden Magic
- Reality manipulation
- Time control
- Soul manipulation

## World Geography

### The Great Tomb of Nazarick
- **Level 1-50**: Lower chambers with basic undead defenders
- **Level 51-99**: Upper chambers with elite guardians
- **Level 100**: The Throne Room - the player's ultimate base

### The Surface World
- **Ruined Cities**: Overgrown metropolitan areas
- **Wilderness**: Untamed lands with monsters
- **Dungeons**: Hidden areas with valuable loot
- **Caves**: Underground networks of ancient passages

### Special Locations
- **The Sanctum of Light**: A holy site under the tomb's control
- **The Abyssal Rift**: A portal to other dimensions
- **The Forge of Souls**: Where undead are created
- **The Library of Eternity**: Contains forbidden knowledge

## Progression

The game progresses through:

1. **Resurrection**: Awakening in the Great Tomb
2. **Expansion**: Building and fortifying the tomb
3. **Conquest**: Securing territories and gathering followers
4. **Ascension**: Becoming an Overlord of the new world
