# Core Gameplay Mechanics

## Core Gameplay Loop

The game operates on a **three-phase gameplay loop**:

### Phase 1: Exploration and Conquest
- Explore dungeons and surface areas
- Discover loot and resources
- Combat enemies and collect souls
- Expand territory through combat

### Phase 2: Base Building and Management
- Build structures in the Great Tomb
- Train and upgrade undead minions
- Manage resources (souls, mana, materials)
- Establish defenses and facilities

### Phase 3: Strategic Warfare
- Command armies in tactical combat
- Plan and execute conquests
- Manage territories and influence
- Engage in larger-scale battles

## Overlord-Style Mechanics

### Undead Minion System
The player commands an army of undead creatures:

- **Minion Management**: Recruit, train, and upgrade undead
- **Command Hierarchy**: Different unit types with specialized roles
- **Minion Loyalty**: Minions gain experience and loyalty to the Overlord
- **Special Abilities**: Each minion type has unique abilities
  - *Skeleton Warriors*: Basic melee fighters
  - *Zombie Hordes*: Swarm tactics and terrain control
  - *Necromancer Captains*: Area damage and debuffs
  - *Wraith Assassins*: Stealth and critical hits

### Dark Abilities
The player character has access to forbidden abilities:
- **Dark Aura**: Damages nearby enemies and heals minions
- **Soul Harvest**: Absorbs enemy souls for power
- **Terrify**: Causes enemies to flee in terror
- **Command Undead**: Direct control of minion AI

## Diablo-Style Mechanics

### Character Progression
- **Level System**: Gain XP through combat and conquest
- **Skill Trees**: Choose from multiple skill paths
  - *Necromancy Tree*: Enhances minion abilities and summoning
  - *Combat Tree*: Improves physical combat skills
  - *Magic Tree*: Enhances spellcasting and dark abilities
  - *Command Tree*: Improves tactical leadership
- **Stat Growth**: Distribute stat points for customized abilities

### Loot System
- **Dungeon Loot**: Weapons, armor, and artifacts found in dungeons
- **Monster Drops**: Valuable items from defeated enemies
- **Treasure Chests**: Hidden rewards in exploration
- **Artifact System**: Special powerful items with unique abilities
- **Rarity Tiers**: Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary items

### Combat System
- **Action Combat**: Real-time combat with keyboard/mouse controls
- **Combo System**: Chain attacks for enhanced damage
- **Critical Hits**: High-risk, high-reward attacks
- **Skill Combos**: Combine abilities for powerful effects
- **Enemy Weaknesses**: Identify and exploit enemy vulnerabilities

## Dungeon Keeper-Style Mechanics

### Base Building
The Great Tomb serves as the player's base with multiple structure types:

- **Living Quarters**: Housing for different minion types
- **Training Grounds**: Train and upgrade undead forces
- **Forge**: Craft weapons and armor
- **Library**: Research new abilities and magic
- **Altars**: Enhance dark powers and summon powerful undead
- **Traps and Defenses**: Protect the tomb from intruders
- **Soul Collection**: Automatically harvest souls from defeated enemies

### Resource Management
- **Souls**: Currency for recruiting and upgrading minions
- **Mana**: Fuel for magic abilities
- **Materials**: Resources for crafting and building
- **Territory**: Expand tomb chambers and conquered lands

### Minion AI
- **Autonomous Action**: Minions act independently within constraints
- **Task Assignment**: Assign specific tasks to minion groups
- **Behavior AI**: Minions patrol, guard, or attack based on orders
- **Group Tactics**: Minions work together for coordinated attacks

## Total War-Style Mechanics

### Strategic Layer
- **Campaign Map**: Strategic view of the world
- **Province System**: Control territories and provinces
- **Resource Distribution**: Strategic resource allocation
- **Diplomacy**: Interact with factions and negotiate

### Army Management
- **Army Composition**: Combine different unit types
- **Unit Roles**: Tanks, damage dealers, support, scouts
- **Special Abilities**: Deploy powerful abilities in battle
- **Customization**: Customize army composition and loadouts

### Tactical Combat
- **Grid-Based Battles**: Turn-based tactical combat system
- **Flanking and Surround**: Tactical advantages in positioning
- **Terrain Effects**: Use terrain to your advantage
- **Morale System**: Enemy and friendly unit morale
- **Command Points**: Limited abilities per turn

## UI/HUD Design

### Main Interface
- **Strategic View**: Campaign map interface
- **Tactical View**: Battle interface
- **Base View**: Tomb management interface
- **Character View**: Player and minion details

### Information Display
- **Minion Panel**: Shows minion status, equipment, abilities
- **Resource Display**: Real-time resource counters
- **Quest Log**: Active and completed quests
- **Map Display**: Dungeon/surface area navigation

### Action Buttons
- **Attack**: Direct combat action
- **Command**: Give orders to minions
- **Build**: Structure construction menu
- **Research**: Ability and technology tree
- **Inventory**: Equipment and item management

### Health and Status
- **Player Health Bar**: Overlord's vitality
- **Minion Health Bars**: Individual and group status
- **Mana/Cooldown Indicators**: Magic resource and ability readiness
- **UI Themes**: Dark interface with gold and silver accents
